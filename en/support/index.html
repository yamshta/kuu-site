Frequently asked questions

Is my voice saved?
No. KUU does not send audio off the device, and temporary audio files are deleted once transcription completes. Only the transcript and classification result are kept.

Does it sync via iCloud?
Yes. Your notes sync to your private iCloud database, accessible only to you. There is no developer-owned server.

How do I cancel KUU+?
Open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → KUU+ → Cancel Subscription. You can also open the same screen from “Manage subscription” inside the KUU app, or via this link. After cancelling, KUU+ stays active until the end of the current period.
